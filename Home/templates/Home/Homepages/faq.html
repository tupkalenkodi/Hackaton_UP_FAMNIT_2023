Frequently Asked Questions


What should I do after registration and login?

After registration and login, follow these steps:

  1. Create your Art Character by answering a series of questions that help the Contexto API understand your art preferences.
  2. On the main working page, select your available Art Character and choose the theme for your content.
  3. Submit the request and wait for Contexto API to generate your customized content.

Can I customize the art content I receive from the API?

Absolutely! Contexto API offers customization options:

You have the ability to customize aspects such as your preferred art forms, styles, emotions, and more. By personalizing these parameters, you'll receive art content tailored to your specific preferences and creative vision.

How long does it take to receive the customized content?

The time it takes to receive your customized content depends on various factors, including the complexity of your request and the current load on the Contexto API. In general, you can expect to receive your content within a few minutes.

Is the generated art content free to use for commercial purposes?

Yes, the generated art content is free to use for commercial purposes. Since the content is generated using your own personalized parameters, you have full ownership and rights over the content produced. Feel free to use it in your commercial projects, exhibitions, or any other context that aligns with your creative vision.

What formats does the generated content come in?

Currently, the generated content is available only in text format. You can receive descriptions, narratives, and textual representations of your personalized art content. However, we are actively working to expand the possibilities, and in the near future, we plan to offer generated content in audio and video formats as well. This expansion will allow you to experience your art content in even more dynamic and engaging ways.

How secure is my personal information?

We take the security of your personal information seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored securely. We only use the information necessary for generating tailored content and strictly adhere to privacy policies to ensure the confidentiality of your data.

Can I request content in multiple languages?

Currently, the Contexto API primarily supports content generation in one primary language. However, we're continuously working to expand our language capabilities, so stay tuned for updates on multi-language support.
